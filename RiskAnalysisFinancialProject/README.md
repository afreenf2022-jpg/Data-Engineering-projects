**🏦Banking Risk Analysis & Customer Insights**

**Analyzing customer financial behavior to identify risk patterns and key banking insights**

**Overview**

This project performs exploratory data analysis (EDA) on banking data to understand customer profiles, financial behavior, and potential risk indicators such as loan exposure and credit usage.

**Tech Stack**

Python (Pandas, NumPy)\
Data Visualization (Matplotlib, Seaborn)

**Key Steps Performed**

Data loading and preprocessing\
Exploratory data analysis (EDA)\
Correlation analysis between financial variables(CLustering)\
Visualization of customer financial patterns\
Identification of risk-related trends

**Key Insights**

Strong relationship between deposits and savings behavior\
Income and age influence financial accumulation patterns\
Distinct separation between business and personal banking activities\
Weak correlation of property ownership with other financial metrics

**Outcome**

Uncovered key drivers of customer financial behavior\
Highlighted potential indicators for risk assessment in banking\
Provided insights to support better financial decision-making.
